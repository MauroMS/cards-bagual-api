Rules & How To Play ⇳

Welcome to the unique Shithead Multiplayer Online game. Here you can play online with your friends and/or enemies, up to 5 players playing together. You can also add "Bot" players so you can play against the computer if you will.

To play the game, choose an unique "game name" and share it with your friends ( Y'all must use the same game name to join the same table ). Also, choose your unique player name, which will be visible to the other players.

To add a "bot player", click on the "turd" icon in the upper right corner once you've joined the game and the game has not yet been started.

During setup stage, you may switch the cards in your hand with your cards visible to the other players. Once all players click "Start", the game begins. The highlighted player has its turn.

Click on "Table" in case you cannot place a card to collect all table cards. Click on "Throw" or drag the card to the table in case you can play the card.

The game uses a set of special cards:

- "2" resets the table, any card can go on top of "2";
- "10" burns the table;
- "3" is transparent, it keeps the value of the card below;

Plus the two special cards "7" which requires a card lower or equal than "7";
and finally "8" which skips the next player.

Furthermore, the table is burned when consecutively four cards with the same number are set on the table.
